Insurance Services

We arrange insurance coverage for all risks irrespective of size or complexity. We are familiar with all the reputable insurance companies and would negotiate the best rate and insurance cover that best suits your needs.

You do not need to pay for our insurance brokerage services. As insurance brokers, our commission is paid by insurance companies and this cost is not transferred to you.

We will help you broker tailor-made insurance solutions to suit your operations.

As an Insurance Broker of choice, we provide among others, the following services to our numerous clients:

  1. Identification and assessment of risks and providing advice on all your insurance needs including analyzing your insurance policies in force.
  2. We will help you design a suitable coverage that will provide the required protection against all insurable risks and liabilities.
  3. We will help you negotiate and obtain competitive premium rates with reliable Insurers within Nigeria and in international markets.
  4. We will follow-up with the issuance of policy certificates, Cover Notes, Endorsements and all the necessary documentation as required by the client.
  5. We provide assistance for the protection of the Client’s interests and ensure that claims are settled as promptly as possible.
  6. We will set up and organize the insurance records of your organization thereby facilitating easy administration.
  7. We provide training for the staff of the insurance division of your company.
We investigate all claims and deal with all the issues associated. We have a stellar track record of negotiating for the most favourable settlement of claims for our clients. Our process is also speedier and more efficient than when an insured works directly with the underwriter for claims settlement. We continuously develop key relationships and requisite logistical processes to ensure that our clients’ claims are promptly settled.
We provide professional advice on pension management for organizations with a view of ensuring that the employees of our clients’ organization have a secured retirement.
